Hi, I'm Davide Cristini.
I'm an Italian software developer.
I'm passionate about programming and open collaboration.

I grew up curios about how technology, story, design and aesthetic can unite to create unique interactive experiences.

During my life I played many milestones of the videogame medium and they had a huge impact on my life and beliefs.

Because of this big influence, I want to take part of what I recon to be the most revolutionary industry in the history of media.

Hobbies

Since 2010, most of my computing happens thanks to Open source software.
In my spare time I like to engage with communities, create tutorials or address issues.

Recently I started the Crystal Bit project.
I'm creating open source tutorials about game and software programming.
Though the videos are for the Italian community, all the source code is in English and it is available on Github.
I started this project creating a series of videos about Godot, an excellent open source game engine.
